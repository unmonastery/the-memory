Overview of the 'The Memory'

This repository has been assembled to provide easy access and friendly navigation to the digital files associated with the BIOS. The repository (if you are reading this on github) is stored on a mini SD card that accompanies the other aspects of the BIOS. If you are reading this in the offline format directly from said mini SD card, you can check for updates by clicking the ribbon in the top left corner.
